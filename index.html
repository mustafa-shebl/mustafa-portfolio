Workout Tips for Beginners

  • Start Slow: Begin with light weights or shorter sessions and gradually increase intensity.
  • Prioritize Form: Focus on proper technique to prevent injuries and target muscles effectively.
  • Set Realistic Goals: Establish achievable short-term goals to stay motivated.
  • Mix It Up: Incorporate a variety of exercises—strength, cardio, flexibility—to keep workouts engaging.
  • Listen to Your Body: Pay attention to how you feel and rest when needed.
